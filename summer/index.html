Coach D

Derek Simmons. I've been coaching basketball for 15 years, starting with my oldest son's first team in California and continuing through every level of youth and high school basketball in Minnesota.

I've coached AAU with Minnesota Heat, D1 Minnesota, and Grassroots Sizzle — where players from my teams went on to play D1, D2, D3, NAIA, and junior college basketball. I was head coach at East Ridge High School, where my freshman team went 17-2. I served as Basketball Board President and Player Development Director (K-12) for the East Ridge Athletic Association, running camps, a summer shot club, and player development programming for 200+ families over two years.

Three-sport athlete in Kansas — state championships in basketball and baseball, and starting quarterback who led the football team to state. My own son was a four-year conference champion, recognized by Prep Hoops as "one of the better shooters on the circuit," and went on to play college basketball.

Beyond skill development, I help players and families navigate the recruiting landscape, including building social media presence and scouting profiles. I've done this for my own players and for the programs I've coached.
