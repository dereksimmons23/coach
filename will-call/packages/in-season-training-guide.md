## MARKET RESEARCH SUMMARY

### Elite Youth Basketball Training Price Ranges (Ages 14-18)

#### Individual Training Sessions
- **Entry Level**: $50-75/hour (high school or college players, group settings)
- **Mid-Tier**: $75-125/hour (experienced coaches, individual attention)
- **Premium**: $125-250/hour (elite credentials, specialized methodology)
- **Elite/Pro**: $250-500+/hour (former pros, celebrity coaches, top-tier facilities)

#### Monthly Package Pricing

**Basic Packages** ($200-400/month):
- 4x 60-minute sessions
- General skill development
- Limited remote support
- Standard coaching methodology

**Premium Packages** ($400-800/month):
- 4-8x 60-90 minute sessions
- Specialized skill development
- Text/email support between sessions
- Video analysis included
- Personalized training plans

**Elite Packages** ($800-1,500+/month):
- Unlimited or extensive session access
- 24/7 coach availability
- Advanced analytics and film study
- Nutrition and conditioning integration
- College recruitment support

## RISK MITIGATION

### What Could Go Wrong & Prevention

**Risk: Athlete doesn't show progress**
- **Prevention**: Set realistic expectations, document small wins, maintain communication
- **Response**: Adjust training approach, increase parent insight into development

**Risk: Payment issues**
- **Prevention**: Auto-pay systems, clear terms, professional invoicing
- **Response**: Friendly reminder system, payment plans when appropriate

**Risk: Schedule conflicts**
- **Prevention**: Consistent time slot, clear rescheduling policy
- **Response**: Flexibility when possible, maintain boundaries

**Risk: Parent dissatisfaction**
- **Prevention**: Clear communication upfront, regular check-ins
- **Response**: Listen actively, provide evidence of progress, adjust approach
