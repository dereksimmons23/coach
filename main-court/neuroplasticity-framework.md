# Basketball Neuroplasticity & Mental Performance Framework
*Integrating neuroscience principles into basketball coaching*

## üß† **CORE NEUROPLASTICITY TRAINING SYSTEM**

### **The "Get 50" Bilateral Assessment Protocol** *(PROVEN EFFECTIVE)*
**Revolutionary Differentiator**: Quantify and systematically develop bilateral skill gaps

#### **Baseline Assessment System** *(Validated in training)*
- **"Get 50" Dominant Hand**: 5 shooting stations, 10 shots each
  - Station 1: Perfect form shots (off-hand behind back)
  - Station 2: Two-ball Mikans 
  - Station 3: Bradleys (bounce 3x, shoot on jump)
  - Station 4: Single-leg balance (shooting leg, opposite foot up)
  - Station 5: Single-leg balance (opposite leg, shooting foot up)
- **Penalty System**: Miss short = non-dominant hand dribble to half-court and back
- **"Get 50" Non-Dominant Hand**: Identical protocol with off-hand
- **Progress Tracking**: Document bilateral gap and improvement rate

**Real Results**: Young athlete went from 23-30/50 to 40/50 dominant hand in 4 weeks. Non-dominant baseline: 23/50. **Neuroplasticity gap identified and quantifiable.**

#### **The Boxing Glove Innovation** *(Game-Changing Discovery)*
- **Constraint as Advantage**: Boxing glove becomes offensive weapon, not restriction
- **Training Focus**: "Offensive boxer" mentality
  - Use glove to create space and protect ball
  - Drive with weak hand while clearing lanes with strong
  - Transform limitation into superpower
- **Mental Breakthrough**: Constraints become confidence builders
- **Application**: Perfect for developing physical presence and weak-hand finishing

#### **Cognitive Load Training Protocol** *(Multi-Modal Excellence)*
- **Bank Shot Drill**: 25 shots each side (dominant hand/side, non-dominant hand/side)
- **Simultaneous Demands**:
  - Mental counting (shot clock awareness)
  - Perfect footwork (no traveling)
  - Communication during chaos
  - Conditioning between shots
- **Reframe**: "Not a shooting drill - mental/footwork/conditioning/communication drill that ends with shooting"
