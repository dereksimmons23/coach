# Pivot & Jab Steps

**Category 10 of 16** | Footwork foundation

---

## Overview

Before you can dribble, shoot, or pass effectively, you need to be able to hold your ground. Pivots and jabs create space and options.

**Progression:**
- All levels work the same fundamental pivots and jabs
- Mastery increases with repetition and game application

---

## Drills

### Left Foot/Forward Pivot
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Fundamental |
| **Balance** | Left foot stays planted, pivot on ball of foot |

**Execution:**
1. Ball in triple threat
2. Left foot is pivot foot (stays planted)
3. Right foot steps forward, rotating body
4. Protect ball, create angle

### Left Foot, Forward Jab Step
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Fundamental |
| **Balance** | Quick, aggressive, return to base |

**Execution:**
1. Ball in triple threat, left foot pivot
2. Jab right foot forward aggressively
3. Read defender's reaction
4. Return to base or attack

## Coaching Notes

**Common Errors:**
- Dragging pivot foot (travel!)
- Jab step too slow (must be explosive)
- Not reading defender after jab
- Rising up during pivot (stay low)

**Cues:**
- "Stay on the ball of your foot"
- "Quick and aggressive"
- "Read the defender's reaction"
- "Make them respect the jab"
