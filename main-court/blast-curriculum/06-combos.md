# Combos

**Category 6 of 16** | Combination moves linking multiple skills

---

## Overview

Combos chain together individual moves into fluid sequences. These are the building blocks for game-speed decision making.

**Progression:**
- **Travel:** Basic 3-4 dribble combos
- **Varsity:** Pound between behind patterns
- **Recruit:** Complex 4-9 dribble sequences with direction changes

---

## Drills

### Pound Between, Back Through - Left Hand
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 3 dribble sequence |
| **Balance** | Big step, little step, feet wide, shoulders over knees |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. 1st dribble: left hand, right foot step between
2. 2nd dribble: right hand back through

### Pound Between Behind, Cross (Alternating)
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 8 dribble sequence / full court |
| **Balance** | Knees 90 degrees, butt down, shoulders back, back straight |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. 1st dribble: left hand pound straight down
2. 2nd dribble: pound between legs
3. 3rd dribble: right hand pound behind back (under butt)
4. 4th dribble: left hand cross to right hand
5. 5th dribble: right hand pound straight down
6. 6th dribble: right hand pound between legs
7. 7th dribble: left hand pound behind back (under butt)
8. 8th dribble: right hand pound
9. Repeat sequence

## Coaching Notes

**Common Errors:**
- Breaking rhythm between moves
- Not staying low through entire sequence
- Rushing instead of being precise
- Losing control on direction changes

**Cues:**
- "Big step, little step"
- "Keep shoulders over knees"
- "Make it flow"
- "Punch dribble: hand and foot together"
