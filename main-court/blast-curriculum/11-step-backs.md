# Step Backs

**Category 11 of 16** | Creating space for jump shots

---

## Overview

The step back creates separation from defenders for a clean look at the basket. Elite scorers master multiple step back variations.

**Progression:**
- **Travel, Varsity, Recruit:** Same techniques with increasing speed and precision
- Key difference: Recruit adds step back combinations (step back, cross, step back)

---

## Drills

### Step Back On Dribble - Right Foot, Jump Shot
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 2 dribble sequence |
| **Balance** | Dipping shoulders, rotating hips, stepping leg through aggressively |

**Modifications:**
- Start in split stance with right foot forward, ball on left hip. As ball hits ground, explode off right foot into shot
- Footwork only

**Execution:**
1. 1st dribble: left hand, right foot step
2. 2nd dribble: left hand, dipping right shoulder
3. Explode off right foot
4. Dribble and step are together like a mini explosion
5. Land with basketball in shooting pocket, wrist bent ready to shoot

### Step Back On Gather - Right Foot, Jump Shot
**Levels:** Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 2 dribble sequence |
| **Balance** | Dipping shoulders, rotating hips, stepping leg through aggressively |

**Modifications:**
- Start in split stance with right foot forward, ball on left hip. Dribble, push basketball out to left side of hip, explode off right foot for jump shot
- Footwork only

**Execution:**
1. 1st dribble: left hand
2. 2nd dribble: left hand, pushing dribble out away from left hip
3. Dip right shoulder
4. Explode off right foot
5. Land with basketball in shooting pocket, wrist bent ready to shoot

### Step Back, Keep Dribble/Cross, Step Back - Right Foot Start
**Levels:** Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 3 dribble sequence |
| **Balance** | Dipping shoulders, rotating hips, stepping leg through aggressively |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. 1st dribble: left hand, right foot step
2. 2nd dribble: left hand crossover to right hand
3. As soon as ball crosses over, rotate hips and step left leg through aggressively
4. 3rd dribble: right hand, dipping left shoulder, exploding off left foot
5. Dribble and step together like a mini explosion
6. Land with basketball in shooting pocket, wrist bent ready to shoot

## Coaching Notes

**Common Errors:**
- Not dipping shoulder (creates separation)
- Stepping before dribble (travel!)
- Landing out of balance
- Not rotating hips aggressively

**Cues:**
- "Dribble and step together like a mini explosion"
- "Dip the shoulder"
- "Rotate hips aggressively"
- "Land in your shooting pocket"
