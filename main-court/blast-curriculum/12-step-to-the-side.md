# Step to the Side

**Category 12 of 16** | Lateral movement for shot creation

---

## Overview

Side steps create lateral separation while maintaining balance for the jump shot. Essential for perimeter scorers.

**Progression:**
- **Travel, Varsity, Recruit:** Same techniques with increasing combinations and speed

---

## Drills

### Side Step, Jump Shot - Left Foot/Right Hand Dribble
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 1 dribble sequence |
| **Balance** | Dipping shoulders, rotating hips, stepping leg through aggressively |

**Modifications:**
- Dribble, hop off left foot landing on right foot, gather basketball
- Dribble, hop off right foot landing on left foot, gather basketball
- Footwork only

**Execution:**
1. 1st dribble: right hand, keeping basketball off right hip
2. Dip left shoulder slightly
3. Load on left leg
4. Explode off left foot and leg as you pound ball with right hand
5. Right foot comes up off ground BEFORE you explode off left foot
6. Land right foot, left foot
7. Gather, jumper, knock it down

## Coaching Notes

**Common Errors:**
- Not loading before exploding
- Feet landing at same time (should be 1-2)
- Not dipping shoulder
- Rushing into shot without balance

**Cues:**
- "Load on your leg before you explode"
- "Right foot comes up BEFORE you explode"
- "Dip the shoulder"
- "Knock it down"
