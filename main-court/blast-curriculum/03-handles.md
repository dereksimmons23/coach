# Handles

**Category 3 of 16** | Stationary ball handling

---

## Overview

Stationary ball handling builds the foundation for everything else. Master these drills before moving to handles on the move.

**Progression:**
- **Travel:** Basic pounds, crosses, between legs, behind back, L-dribble
- **Varsity:** 2-ball work, complex patterns, catch-and-pass combos
- **Recruit:** Advanced coordination drills

**Standard Format:** Most drills are 45 seconds with a stamina focus. "LET IT BURN."

---

## Drills

### Ankle Dribble (Training Aid: Drum Pads & Sticks)
**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 45 seconds / stamina |
| **Balance** | Knees 90 degrees, butt down, shoulders back, back straight |

**Modifications:**
- 5 reps
- 7 reps
- 10 reps

**Execution:**
1. Feet outside shoulders
2. Butt at 90 degrees as if in an imaginary chair
3. Shoulders back, chest forward
4. Head up
5. Hand without ball at your side with elbow bent
6. DO NOT rest hand on knee or thigh
7. LET IT BURN

### Cross - Alternating
**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 45 seconds / stamina |
| **Balance** | Knees 90 degrees, butt down, shoulders back, back straight |

**Modifications:**
- 3 pound dribbles, then cross / repeat
- 2 pound dribbles, then cross / repeat
- 1 pound dribble, then cross / repeat

**Execution:**
1. Swing cross WIDE, letting it float out away from your body
2. Elbow almost locks out to straight
3. Shift your weight to sway in the direction you are crossing
4. Remember: dribbling is about RHYTHM

### Pound Behind - Alternating

### Front Cross (Left & Right Hand)
**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 45 seconds / stamina |
| **Balance** | Knees 90 degrees, butt down, shoulders back, back straight |

**Modifications:**
- Continuous dribbling, moving ball from left to right
- 2 pound dribbles into front cross, then 2 pound dribbles front cross back
- 1 pound dribble into front cross, then 1 pound dribble front cross back

**Execution:**
1. Keep inside of bicep close to rib cage
2. Pound basketball and sway shoulders/torso in direction ball is moving
3. When you change directions with front cross, your hand will always be tilted toward outside of body
4. THIS IS NOT AN INSIDE OUT DRIBBLE

---

### Front Cross - Alternating 3's
**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 45 seconds / stamina |
| **Balance** | Knees 90 degrees, butt down, shoulders back, back straight |

**Modifications:**
- 1, 2, 3, front cross and catch / repeat other hand

**Execution:**
1. Keep inside of bicep close to rib cage
2. Start with front cross going toward center of body
3. 3 front crosses
4. On the third cross, switch hands
5. Execute 3 more front crosses starting toward center of body
6. Sway shoulders/torso in direction ball is moving
7. THIS IS NOT AN INSIDE OUT DRIBBLE

### Inside Out Dribble - Left Hand
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 45 seconds / stamina |
| **Balance** | Knees 90 degrees, butt down, shoulders back, back straight |

**Modifications:**
- Tennis ball inside out dribble catch
- 3 dribbles inside out / repeat
- 1 dribble inside out / repeat

**Execution:**
1. Keep inside of bicep close to rib cage
2. Pound the dribble
3. As it bounces back up, tilt left hand pinky down
4. Push basketball toward center of chest
5. Tilt left hand thumb down toward right arm
6. Push basketball away from chest back to outside of body
7. ALL OF THIS HAPPENS WITHIN ONE DRIBBLE

### 2 Steps Forward, 2 Steps Back (Both Sides)
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 10 dribble sequence / 45 seconds / stamina |
| **Balance** | Knees 90 degrees, butt down, FEET WIDE APART, shoulders back, back straight |

**Modifications:**
- 2 dribbles in between each sequence
- 1 dribble in between each sequence

**Execution:**
1. 1st dribble: left foot forward step, right hand between legs
2. 2nd dribble: right foot forward step, left hand behind back
3. 3rd dribble: left foot step back, right hand back through
4. 4th dribble: right foot step back, left hand crossover
5. 5th dribble: right hand CROSSOVER AGAIN (brings you to other side)
6. 6th-10th: repeat sequence starting with right foot forward

## Coaching Notes

**Common Errors:**
- Resting hand on knee (builds bad habits)
- Standing up during sequences (stay low!)
- Rushing through instead of focusing on technique
- Not engaging core

**Cues:**
- "Knees 90 degrees, butt down"
- "LET IT BURN" (embrace the conditioning)
- "Shoulders back, chest forward, head up"
- "Dribbling is about RHYTHM"
