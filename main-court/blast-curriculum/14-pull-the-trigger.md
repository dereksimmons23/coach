# Pull the Trigger

**Category 14 of 16** | Shooting drills

---

## Overview

Shooters shoot. These drills build form, rhythm, and game-situation shooting mechanics.

**Progression:**
- **Travel, Varsity, Recruit:** Form shooting with increasing complexity (off screens, fades)

---

## Drills

### Form Shooting With Guide Hand
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | T3 / V6 / R9 (Travel: 3 swishes, Varsity: 6 swishes, Recruit: 9 swishes) |
| **Balance** | Feet shoulder width, elbow underneath wrist, shoot with finger pads NOT palm |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. Start close to basket
2. Elbow under wrist
3. Guide hand on side of ball (not pushing)
4. Shoot with finger pads, not palm
5. Range appropriate - move back as you master distance
6. Count swishes, not makes

---

### Curl Off Pin Down (Curling Right) - Sidestep
**Levels:** Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Sidestep, catch and shoot |
| **Balance** | "Bury" or "Setup" your defender before curling |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. Defender is chasing hip pocket
2. Set your defender up
3. DON'T LEAVE SPACE for defender to squeeze through
4. Curl off pin down screen (rub shoulders with your teammate)
5. Sidestep footwork into catch: explode off left foot
6. Catch ball while landing right, left
7. Land with great balance, feet under shoulders
8. Shot

---

### Curl Off Pin Down (Curling Right) - Sidestep "Mini Fade"
**Levels:** Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Sidestep, fade, catch and shoot |
| **Balance** | "Bury" or "Setup" your defender before curling |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. Defender is chasing hip pocket
2. Set your defender up
3. DON'T LEAVE SPACE for defender to squeeze through
4. Curl off pin down screen (rub shoulders with your teammate)
5. Sidestep footwork into catch: explode off left foot
6. Catch ball while landing right, left
7. Land with great balance but mini fade backwards
8. Create more space for yourself
9. Feet slightly behind shoulders as you land and catch
10. As you fade, focus on back of rim and get basketball in the air

### Flare Off Pin Down
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Flare, catch and shoot |
| **Balance** | "Bury" or "Setup" your defender before curling |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. Defender gets caught under screen
2. Set your defender up
3. DON'T LEAVE SPACE for defender to squeeze through
4. Start to curl
5. Recognize if your defender gets caught going under the screen
6. Use your teammate's screen and flare
7. Put yourself between your screen and your defender
8. Recognize and make your reads QUICKLY

## Coaching Notes

**Common Errors:**
- Pushing with guide hand
- Shooting with palm instead of finger pads
- Not setting up defender before using screen
- Leaving space for defender to squeeze through

**Cues:**
- "Elbow under wrist"
- "Finger pads, not palm"
- "9 is the standard" (swishes)
- "Rub shoulders with your teammate"
- "Set your defender up"
- "Focus on back of rim"
