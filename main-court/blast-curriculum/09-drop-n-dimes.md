# Drop 'N' Dimes

**Category 9 of 16** | Passing drills

---

## Overview

Great scorers can pass. Great passers create open shots. This category builds passing fundamentals and game-situation reads.

**Progression:**
- **Travel:** Fundamental passes (chest, bounce, backdoor, post feed)
- **Varsity:** One-handed passes, off-the-dribble passes, thread the needle
- **Recruit:** Post entry variations, pocket passes, back passes

---

## Drills

### Two Handed Chest Pass
**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Fundamental |
| **Balance** | Feet shoulder width, step into pass |

**Execution:**
1. Ball at chest level
2. Thumbs behind ball, fingers spread
3. Step toward target
4. Extend arms, snap wrists
5. Thumbs point down on follow through

---

### Two Handed Bounce Pass
**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Fundamental |
| **Balance** | Feet shoulder width, step into pass |

**Execution:**
1. Ball at chest level

**Levels:** Travel, Varsity

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Game situation |
| **Balance** | Read the cut, lead the receiver |

**Execution:**

| **Balance** | Read post position |

## Coaching Notes

**Common Errors:**
- Telegraphing passes (no wind up!)
- Not stepping into passes
- Passing to where player is, not where they're going
- Not protecting the ball before passing

**Cues:**
- "Put it in their shooting pocket"
- "Lead the receiver"
- "Quick release, no wind up"
- "See the window, hit the window"
