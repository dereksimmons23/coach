# Handles on the Move

**Category 4 of 16** | Ball handling while moving

---

## Overview

Stationary handles build the foundation. Handles on the move apply those skills to game situations where you're attacking, retreating, and changing direction.

**Progression:**
- **Travel:** Speed dribble, front cross in motion, basic combos
- **Varsity:** Inside out in motion, punch drag
- **Recruit:** Advanced wrap combos, underdrag, drop cross

---

## Drills

### Speed Dribble - Left Hand
**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 6 dribbles for full court layup |
| **Balance** | Under control, off hand bent with bicep close to ribs |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. Find your rhythm
2. Push ball out in front
3. Cover ground with each dribble
4. Maintain control at top speed

### Front Cross - Left Hand (In Motion)
**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | In motion |
| **Balance** | Feet outside shoulders, shoulders over knees |

**Modifications:**
- 2 dribbles on each side
- One continuous dribble from outside left foot to outside right foot

**Execution:**
1. Keep inside of bicep close to rib cage
2. Pound basketball and sway shoulders/torso in direction ball is moving
3. When you change directions with front cross, hand always tilted toward outside of body
4. THIS IS NOT AN INSIDE OUT DRIBBLE

### Front Cross Alternating (In Motion)
**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | In motion |
| **Balance** | Feet outside shoulders, shoulders over knees |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. Keep inside of bicep close to rib cage
2. Start with front cross going toward center of body
3. 3 front crosses
4. On third cross, switch hands and execute 3 more front crosses
5. Sway shoulders/torso in direction ball is moving
6. THIS IS NOT AN INSIDE OUT DRIBBLE

### Between Cross Back - Right Hand (In Motion)
**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 45 seconds / stamina |
| **Balance** | Knees 90 degrees, butt down, shoulders back, back straight |

**Modifications:**
- Footwork only
- 6 dribble sequence (between on 3, cross on 6)

**Execution:**
1. 1st dribble: right hand, stepping out with left leg at same time going between leg
2. 2nd dribble: left hand cross back to right hand
3. Repeat sequence

### Retreat Dribble (Right & Left Hand)
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 6 dribble sequence |
| **Balance** | Wide base, looking over forward shoulder |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. Left hand retreat dribbles
2. Load and push off right foot with each retreat
3. Think of this as a defensive shuffle with your dribble
4. Look over your right shoulder as you retreat backwards with left hand
5. Basketball and right foot hit the ground at the same time
6. Load and explode together

### Pound Wrap Alternating
**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | In motion |
| **Balance** | Feet outside shoulders, shoulders over knees |

**Modifications:**
- Stationary: right hand pound, wrap right hand
- Stationary: left hand pound, wrap left hand

**Execution:**
1. 1st dribble: pound left hand between
2. 2nd dribble: right hand wrap behind back
3. Footwork on wrap: push off right leg
4. Before right leg touches left leg, left leg pushes forward and out wide

### Pound Behind, Drop Cross Underneath
**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | In motion |
| **Balance** | Feet outside shoulders, shoulders over knees |

**Modifications:**
- Pound behind back off wall and catch (right and left hand)
- 3 pounds then behind back
- 2 pounds then behind back

**Execution:**
1. Pound dribble hard
2. Keep height of dribble below your butt
3. Cross behind back
4. Remember: cross should be UNDER YOUR BUTT
5. Repeat on other side

**Levels:** Travel

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | In motion |
| **Balance** | Feet outside shoulders, shoulders over knees |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**

2. Mini jump stop
3. While in the air, rotate hips in direction you are going
4. Repeat process on other side
5. Keep dribble around knee height

### Inside Out - Left Hand (In Motion)
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | In motion |
| **Balance** | Feet outside shoulders, shoulders over knees |

**Modifications:**
- Tennis ball inside out dribble catch
- 3 dribbles inside out / repeat
- 1 dribble inside out / repeat

**Execution:**
1. Keep inside of bicep close to rib cage
2. Pound the dribble
3. As it bounces back up, tilt left hand pinky down
4. Push basketball toward center of chest
5. Tilt left hand thumb down toward right arm
6. Push basketball away from chest back to outside of body
7. ALL OF THIS HAPPENS WITHIN ONE DRIBBLE

### Punch Drag Alternating
**Levels:** Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | In motion / 5 dribble sequence |
| **Balance** | Feet outside shoulders, shoulders over knees |

**Modifications:**
- Step out with one dribble right hand, right foot STOP TOGETHER
- Step out with one dribble left hand, left foot STOP TOGETHER

**Execution:**
1. 1st dribble: right hand, left foot step
2. 2nd dribble: right hand, right foot step
3. 3rd dribble: cross
4. 4th dribble: left hand, right foot step
5. 5th dribble: left hand, left foot step

**Levels:** Recruit

|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | In motion |
| **Balance** | Feet outside shoulders, shoulders over knees |

**Modifications:**
- Stationary
- One dribble into drop cross

**Execution:**
1. 1st dribble: right hand cross to left hand as you step out diagonally with right foot
2. 2nd dribble: left hand - explode off right foot pushing HARD to the left

4. Repeat sequence on other side

## Coaching Notes

**Common Errors:**
- Standing up while moving (stay low!)
- Not covering ground with dribbles
- Losing control at speed
- Retreat dribble without looking over shoulder

**Cues:**
- "Feet outside shoulders"
- "Shoulders over knees"
- "Load and explode together"
- "Find your rhythm"
