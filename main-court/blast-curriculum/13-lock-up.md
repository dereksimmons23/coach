# Lock Up

**Category 13 of 16** | Defensive drills

---

## Overview

Defense wins championships. These drills build the conditioning, positioning, and quick-twitch muscles needed to guard anyone.

**Progression:**
- **Varsity, Recruit:** Isometric holds and defensive shuffle patterns

---

## Drills

### 90 Degrees for 90 Seconds - Ball Above Head
**Levels:** Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | 90 seconds / stamina |
| **Balance** | Knees 90 degrees, butt down, shoulders back, back straight |

**Modifications:** None (Varsity does 45 seconds with ball out front)

**Execution:**
1. Feet outside your shoulders
2. Butt down, back straight
3. Elbows locked out with basketball above head
4. Head up, looking forward
5. KEEP YOUR HEAD UP AND BE A STATUE
6. Don't move a muscle
7. LET IT BURN

---

### Defensive Shuffle/Slide - Left 1, Right 2, Left 3, Long Aggressive Slide
**Levels:** Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Full court / stamina |
| **Balance** | Knees 90 degrees, butt down, shoulders back, back straight |

**Modifications:**
- 3 slides per direction
- 6 slides per direction

**Execution:**
1. Defensive stance - stay low
2. Shuffle left 1 time
3. Shuffle right 2 times
4. Shuffle left 3 times
5. On the last defensive shuffle: EXPLODE as hard as you can
6. Lunge out aggressively to stop opponent's progress to the basket
7. LET IT BURN

## Coaching Notes

**Common Errors:**
- Standing up during shuffles (stay low!)
- Clicking heels together (feet never touch)
- Not exploding on final slide
- Head dropping during isometric hold

**Cues:**
- "Knees 90 degrees, butt down"
- "LET IT BURN"
- "EXPLODE on the last one"
- "Be a statue"
- "Feet never touch"
