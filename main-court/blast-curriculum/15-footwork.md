# Footwork

**Category 15 of 16** | Movement patterns and foundations

---

## Overview

Great footwork is the foundation of everything. These drills build the movement patterns that make skills possible.

**Progression:**
- **Travel, Varsity, Recruit:** Same movement patterns with increasing speed and precision

---

## Drills

### Stutter
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Stutter / full court |
| **Balance** | Wide base, feet outside shoulders, stay low |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. Quick choppy feet
2. Stay low throughout
3. You should hear your shoes squeaking on the gym floor
4. LET IT BURN

---

### Big Step, Little Step
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Big step, little step / full court |
| **Balance** | Wide base, feet outside shoulders |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. Start in low triple threat stance with right foot forward
2. Step out diagonally BIG with right foot
3. With left foot, take a small LITTLE step forward
4. Your right foot and left foot will NEVER meet or be parallel with each other
5. Maintain separation between feet at all times

---

### Punch Drag
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Rip punch drag / full court |
| **Balance** | Wide base, feet outside shoulders |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. Start in triple threat, right foot pivot
2. Rip through, stepping left foot through
3. Step right foot through
4. Hard STOP on right foot, keeping left foot behind you
5. Now triple threat, left foot pivot
6. Rip through, stepping right foot through
7. Step left foot through
8. Hard STOP on left foot, keeping right foot behind you
9. Repeat sequence

---

### Load, Explode, Glide, Drag, Plant
**Levels:** Travel, Varsity, Recruit

| Component | Details |
|-----------|---------|
| **Sequence** | Load, explode, glide, drag, plant / full court |
| **Balance** | Wide base, feet outside shoulders |

**Modifications:** None

**Execution:**
1. Wide triple threat stance without basketball
2. Load on your left foot
3. Explode off left foot, lifting right foot at same time
4. Glide out diagonally, dragging your left foot
5. Plant and hard stop on right foot, keeping left foot behind you
6. Now load on your right foot
7. Explode off right foot, lifting left foot at same time
8. Glide out diagonally, dragging your right foot
9. Plant and hard stop on left foot, keeping right foot behind you
10. Repeat sequence

## Coaching Notes

**Common Errors:**
- Standing up during movement (stay low!)
- Feet coming together (maintain separation)
- Not loading before exploding
- Rushing through patterns

**Cues:**
- "Wide base, feet outside shoulders"
- "You should hear your shoes squeaking"
- "Load and explode"
- "Feet never come together"
- "LET IT BURN"
