## **RISK MITIGATION**

### **What Could Go Wrong & How to Prevent It**

**Risk: Athlete doesn't show progress**
- **Prevention**: Set realistic expectations upfront, document small wins, maintain parent communication
- **Response**: Adjust training approach, increase communication, offer parents insight into long-term development

**Risk: Payment issues or late payments**
- **Prevention**: Auto-pay systems, clear payment terms, professional invoicing
- **Response**: Friendly reminder system, late fee structure, willingness to work out payment plans

**Risk: Schedule conflicts or cancellations**
- **Prevention**: Consistent weekly time slot, clear rescheduling policy, makeup session system
- **Response**: Flexibility when possible, maintain boundaries, document patterns

**Risk: Parent dissatisfaction or unrealistic expectations**
- **Prevention**: Clear communication upfront, regular check-ins, manage expectations
- **Response**: Listen actively, provide evidence of progress, adjust approach if needed, graceful exit option

**Risk: Athlete loses motivation or commitment**
- **Prevention**: Make training engaging, celebrate progress, maintain relationship
- **Response**: Connect with parents, adjust training approach, consider if relationship is good fit
