# COACH.CLAUDEWILL.IO - Project Handoff

## Core Decision: Repository, Not Platform

**What it is:** A place where Coach D's basketball tools and resources naturally accumulate over time.

**What it's not:** A business to run, platform to maintain, or massive 2026 launch commitment.

**The model:** 
- Build tools when you need them (like today's lineup builder)
- Document resources as you create them (Tim's curriculum, training plans)
- Organize them in one discoverable place
- Let it grow organically

**Philosophy:** "We built an app that works in a few hours. It would have taken nearly as long to find a PDF and fill it in. That's the whole game."
