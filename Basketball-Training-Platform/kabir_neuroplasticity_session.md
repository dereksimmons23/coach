### **Phase 2: Effortful Experience Ladder** *(20 minutes)*

#### **Progressive Discomfort Shooting Protocol**
**Penalty System**: Miss 2 shots in a row = Sprint to half-court and back (left-hand dribble only)

1. **Level 1: Foundation** *(5 minutes)*
   - Left-hand shots from 3 feet
   - **Goal**: Build basic success pattern
   - **Mental Focus**: "I can do this, my brain is learning"

2. **Level 2: Pressure** *(5 minutes)*
   - Left-hand shots with defensive hand in face
   - **Discomfort Increase**: Visual pressure + awkward hand
   - **Coaching**: "Embrace the uncomfortable feeling"

3. **Level 3: Physical Contact** *(5 minutes)*
   - Left-hand shots after receiving contact/bump
   - **Discomfort Peak**: Physical + skill challenge combined
   - **Mental Performance**: "This is exactly where growth happens"

4. **Level 4: Game Situation** *(5 minutes)*
   - "Game-winning" left-hand shots with crowd noise/pressure
   - **Maximum Discomfort**: All elements combined
   - **Reframing**: "Elite players thrive in these moments"

#### **Sprint Penalty Documentation**
- **Track**: How many penalties triggered
- **Observe**: Mental state during/after sprints
- **Coach**: Use physical discomfort as mental training opportunity
