---

## PROGRAM PHILOSOPHY

In-season training isn't about adding more — it's about **optimizing what's already there**. While school basketball demands peak performance, our focus is on:

1. **Complementary Development** - Skills that enhance school team success without conflicting with coaching systems
2. **Recovery & Longevity** - Managing fatigue, preventing injury, extending career trajectory  
3. **Mental Performance** - Game preparation, film study, decision-making under pressure
4. **Individual Identity** - Developing signature skills that separate you from competition

> *"The best players don't practice harder during the season — they practice smarter."* - Coach D

## FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

**Q: How does this work with school basketball?**  
A: We complement, never conflict. Sessions are scheduled around school practice/games and focus on skills that enhance school team performance.

**Q: What if we have a heavy tournament week?**  
A: Flexibility is built-in. Sessions adjust based on game load — lighter skill work, more mental prep and recovery.

**Q: How do we handle injuries or illness?**  
A: Sessions pause, text support continues. We focus on mental preparation, film study, and recovery protocols. No wasted investment.

**Q: Can we pause/resume if needed?**  
A: Yes. Life happens. We build around your schedule with transparent communication.

**Q: What happens after April?**  
A: We create a comprehensive summer plan. Option to continue training or transition to summer programming.

**Q: How is this different from regular training?**  
A: In-season is about optimization, not overload. We manage fatigue, prevent burnout, and develop game-ready skills without adding stress.
