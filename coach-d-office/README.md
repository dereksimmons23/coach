## Values & Approach

**What Coach D believes:**
- Every player deserves good coaching
- Development takes time and patience
- Mental game matters as much as physical skills
- Joy in the work creates sustainable progress
- Basketball can be an extension of who you are

**What you can expect:**
- Honest assessment of skill level and potential
- Realistic development timelines
- Clear communication with players and parents
- Hard work with genuine encouragement
- Focus on long-term growth, not quick fixes

**What Coach D won't do:**
- Promise college scholarships or NBA careers
- Take shortcuts with player development
- Work with disrespectful players or parents
- Prioritize winning over player growth
- Compromise safety or ethics
